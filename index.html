Forest of riddles

-You wake up in a dark forest. Not a good place to be in. You should probably try to get out. Luckily, you see a road.

-A strange creature with the head of a human and the body of a lion and with bird wings emerges from the forest. “Greetings traveler. I'm a sphinx. Solve my riddle or I will kill you

-Sphinx continues: “Which creature has one voice and yet becomes four-footed and two-footed and three-footed?”

Correct! You can proceed

You walk some more. You hear the sound of rushing water and soon you arrive at a river. Luckily, there's a bridge. Unluckily, there's a strange creature guarding it.

-As you step cautiously on the bridge, the creature notices you. “I'm a bridge troll. Solve my riddle or I will bite your head off.”

“I always run but never walk, often murmur, never talk, have a bed but never sleep, have a mouth but never eat. Who am I?”

That's right. Good job.

You managed to get out of the forest. Congratulations!
